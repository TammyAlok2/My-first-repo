THE SEVEN WONDERS OF THE WORLD

The 7 wonders of the World are :

  1. The Taj Mahal - India
  2. Colosseum - Italy
  3. Chicken Itza - Mexico
  4. Machu Picchu - Peru
  5. Chirst the Redeemer - Brazil
  6. Petra - Jordan
  7. Great Wall of the china - China

The Great Pyramid of the Giza has also been added to the list, but not included in the 7 wonders


TAJ MAHAL, INDIA


The Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is well known across the world for its historical value, its tale of love, and its stunning
beauty. The Taj Mahal is located in the historic Indian city of Agra. It houses the tomb of Mumtaz Mahal,
the wife of the Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan. It is said that the Emperor loved his wife dearly and was
prompted to build the Taj Mahal after her death as a testament to his love.


COLOSSEUM , ITALY


COLOSSEUM , ITALY

Rome, Italy, hosts the Colosseum, which is one of the world's seven wonders. Sometimes called the Flavian Amphitheatre,
the Colosseum is an oval-shaped amphitheater in the center of the city. Constructed from concrete and sand, it is the largest
in the world. The Colosseum's construction was initiated in AD 72 by Emperor Vespasian and was finished by AD 80 by his
, Titus. Domitian, another emperor of the Flavian dynasty later made certain modifications to the amphitheater.
The labor of tens of thousands of slaves were used to build this magnificent structure.


CHICKEN ITZA ,MEXICO


CHICKEN ITZA ,MEXICO

Chicken itza is an archaeological site located in Mexico’s Yucatán State. It is a pre-Columbian city that was built during the
Terminal Classic period by the Maya people. Chichen Itza's structures like the temples, arcades, and pyramids were sacred
to the ancient Maya people. Chicken Itza is believed to have been one of the major cities of the ancient Mayan world and
constructions in the city exhibit a variety of architectural styles.


MACHU PICHU , PERU


MACHU PICHU , PERU

Machu Pichu is a great representation of the Incan way of life. The positions of important structures in Machu Pichu were
influenced by the location of nearby mountains regarded as holy by the Inca people. No mortar was used to build the structures
in Machu Pichu.The stones were cut and wedged in a manner that even a credit card would not go through the joints. While
it definitely improved the aesthetic value of the buildings, it also protected from earthquakes. The stones would 'dance'
if there were earthquakes and then fall back into place once the tremors subsided.


CHIRST- THE REDEEMER, BRAZIL


CHIRST- THE REDEEMER, BRAZIL

One of Brazil's most iconic symbols, the Art Deco styled statue of Jesus chirst in Rio de Jenerio is one of the seven wonders of
the world. The credit of building the statue goes to Paul Landowski, a French sculptor. The Romanian sculptor, Gheorghe Leonida,
was responsible for fashioning the face. The sculpture cost a total of $250,000 which was donated by individuals from in and around Brazil.


PETRA , JORDON


PETRA, JORDAN

Petra Jordan is included in the 7 wonders of the world . Petra is also nicknamed as the “Rose City” due to the stone color from
which it is carved. It has immense archaeological, historical, and architectural value that makes it a jaw-dropping tourist attraction.
The water conduit system and the rock-cut architecture are the two most notable features of this ancient city.
The water conduit system installed by the ancient Nabataeans helped give rise to a flourishing city in a desert. The Royal Tombs which
are large mausoleums with impressive facades carved into rock are a major attraction in Petra.


THE GREAT WALL OF THE CHINA, CHINA


THE GREAT WALL OF THE CHINA

The Great wall of the china is a global tourist hotspot, is known across the world for its uniqueness, great length, and historical value.
It is also considered to be one of the seven wonders of the world. Great wall of china is associated with thousands of years of Chinese history.
